# 🌿 Wild Plants — where to find them

Each drug plant only spawns in certain biomes, and only in **newly-explored chunks** (~1 patch per **20 chunks**). Go to fresh, never-loaded areas of the right biome.

| Plant | Biomes | Search JEI |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| 🌿 **Weed** | **Jungle** (jungle, sparse jungle, bamboo jungle) | weed |
| 🍃 **Coca** | **Windswept hills / gravelly hills / windswept forest** | coca |
| 🌺 **Poppy** | **Plains, sunflower plains, meadow** | poppy |
| 🚬 **Tobacco** | **Savanna** (all savanna types) | tobacco |
| 🍄 **Shrooms** | **Dark forest, forest, birch forest, flower forest, swamp, mangrove swamp** | shroom |

Wild plants grow in small **patches** — when you find one, look around, there are usually more.

---

# 🌱 Farming the plants

1. **Break a wild plant** → get **seeds**.
2. **Plant seeds** on **tilled dirt** (hoe the ground), with light and water like normal crops.
3. Let it **fully grow** (bonemeal speeds it up). Growth stages: weed/coca/tobacco = 5 stages (slower); poppy/shrooms = 3 stages (faster).
4. **Harvest a mature plant** → you get the **product + 1 seed back** (to replant) + a **5% chance of 1 bonus seed**.

*(Farming is balanced — 1 seed per harvest so you always have enough to replant, plus a rare bonus.)*

# 🧪 Create: Broken Bad — cook meth (Breaking Bad style)

This addon adds a full **meth-cooking chemistry chain** using Create machines. Two ways to start:

### The ingredients / routes
- **Grow Ephedra:** plant **Ephedra Seeds** → harvest **Ephedra** → refine into **Pseudoephedrine**.
- **Or the Sudafed route:** **Box of Sudafed → Sudafed → Pseudoephedrine**.

### The chemistry (via Create mixers/basins/heat)
Pseudoephedrine + reagents → **Methamphetamine** → crystallize on a **Tray** → **Blue Methamphetamine** (the good stuff).

Reagents you'll craft/handle: **Red & White Phosphorus, Iodine, Methylamine, Phenylacetone, Phenylacetic Acid, Acetic Anhydride, Ammonia, Methanol, Brine, Cyanide,** plus catalysts (**Aluminosilicate, Copper-Zinc**) and gases (**Hydrogen, Oxygen, Nitrogen**).

**How to learn it:** search **"Methamphetamine"** or **"Blue Methamphetamine"** in JEI and follow the recipe tree backwards — it shows every step from Ephedra/Sudafed to the final blue crystal. It's meant to be a **multi-step Create factory** — build it like any Create contraption.

# 🥃 DinoBriks Rum — brewery (50+ drinks)

A brewing system built around an **Aging Barrel**.

### How it works
1. **Craft the Aging Barrel** (search `aging barrel` in JEI — it's the one craftable item; everything else is made *in* the barrel).
2. **Ferment** base ingredients (sugar cane, wheat, potatoes, cactus, berries, fern, seeds…) into a raw brew.
3. **Distil** it (for spirits).
4. **Age** it in the Aging Barrel — the **wood type** (oak, birch, cherry, dark oak, crimson, chorus, bamboo…) and the **aging time** (2, 3, 4, 5, 8, 12, 16 days) change the result.

### Drink families you can make
**Beer · Wine · Rum · Vodka · Whiskey · Tequila · Absinthe** — plus many special variants depending on wood + time (e.g. copper rum, pumpkin beer, and more — 50+ total).

Different wood + different aging days = different premium drinks. Experiment!
